Lunges

Description

Lunges are a lower body exercise that work similar muscles to the squat with a different range of motion.

Primary Muscles Involved

  • Hamstrings
  • Glutes
  • Quadriceps

Procedure

The exercise can be performed with just the bodyweight or with dumbbells in each hand. While a heavy weight can be used, it is best to be cautious due to the balance and coordination required for the exercise. Otherwise, the movement is relatively simple.

  1. Step forward or back with one foot.
  2. Lower the hips to bend the front leg while dropping the knee in the back leg.
  3. Push up and return to a standing position.

Variations

The variations in lunges allow for greater weight to be used and require less balance and coordination.

Bulgarian Split Squat

Place the back leg on an elevated surface, preferably a bench. Hold dumbbells in hand or a barbell over the shoulder. Perform steps two and three of the regular lunge movement.
