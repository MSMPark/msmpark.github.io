Three Dice

Premise

A set of three weighted dice is observed. Each die represents its respective state. They are weighted such that the first has a 50% chance of rolling a 1, the second has a 50% chance of rolling a 3, and the third has a 50% chance of rolling a six.

  • When you roll a 1 or a 2 on any die, the first die is rolled next.

  • When you roll a 3 or a 4 on any die, the second die is rolled next.

  • When you roll a 5 or a 6 on any die, the third die is rolled next.
