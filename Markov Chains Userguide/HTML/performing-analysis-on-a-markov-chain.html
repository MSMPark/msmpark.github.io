Examining properties of a Markov Chain allows you to determine key features of a scenario such as steady state, and the time it takes to reach the steady state. The steady state is the moment when the probabilities for transitioning to each state are identical to the one another. It predicts, after a certain amount of time, the chance of the system ending on a certain state. You can determine these features through the methods below:

  1. Using eigenvalues and eigenvectors
  2. Using iterative methods.
  3. Finding ratios of appearances.
