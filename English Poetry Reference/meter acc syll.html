Meter

Meter Type (Accented Syllabic)

Description

The type of meter that determines the number of feet per line. Determines line breaks in a poem.

Types

Name # of Feet per line Examples (iambic)
Monometer 1 a foot
Dimeter 2 a broken foot
Trimeter 3 the lady's broken foot
Tetrameter 4 I saw a lady break her foot
Pentameter 5 I think I saw a lady break her foot
Hexameter 6 A scream! I think I saw a lady break her foot
Heptameter 7 I've dragged this image through the dirt: a lady's broken foot
